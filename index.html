Shift Schedule Analyzer

Import schedule data from the clipboard to check rest between shifts and consecutive-day streaks.

Input Data

Use the bookmarklet on WMT Schedules → Edit Non Published, then click Parse from Clipboard (or press Ctrl+V on this page). Shift times use the official ZOB Shift Legend (127 codes). Mid shifts (e.g. 2230, 2100) start the evening before their schedule column date.

Bookmarklet — Grab Data from a Webpage

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar. On a schedule page, click it to copy table data to your clipboard, then return here and click "Parse from Clipboard".
